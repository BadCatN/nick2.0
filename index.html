History of A Personal Point of View, LLC

From Our Founder Margo Jones

APPOV, LLC also known as A Personal Point of View, LLC is an Creative Consulting firm that has been in business for over 29 years and has established great ties in the community in Seattle and worldwide. Margo Jones the owner has made great contributions as an Entrepreneur, Community Leader, and Mentor. Working with some of the world’s largest Corporations in the industries of the Entertainment, Fashion, and Community nonprofits. APPOV has worked with organizations such as Nordstrom, Eddie Bauer, Voda Studios, Macy’s, Beverly Boy Productions, Goodwill, Washington State Lottery, VH1, Seattle Youth and Violence Initiative, Coyote Central, Africatown PDA, Capitol Hill Housing, Central Area Collaborative, Vulcan, Umoja Peace Center and the Historic MOHAI Museum specializing in a variety of professional business services within the businesses of Fashion, Entertainment, Film, Community Outreach, Event Planning, Photography, and Talent representative for other creatives in the fields of wardrobe styling, hair and makeup and modeling. APPOV works in all aspects of creation, implementation, logistical planning, and production of specialized events. APPOV provides services to our clients that are professional, innovative, honest and memorable. We set standards of excellence for our clients to help them to achieve their desired goals and work to make sure the process is seamless so they may rest assure that when they’re working with us the implementation of success is guaranteed.
The Fashion Collaboratory is where APPOV is stationed and where the magic of creation takes place. It’s located at 2917 Rainier Ave S. Suite 104 and is nestled in the heart of Seattle’s Rainier Valley inside the Mt. Baker Art Lofts. This beautiful and contemporary full service business studio is where business professionals create and collaborate by building and working on projects productions in all phases. From hosting private and Community special events for clients both of the professional and personal nature along with creating amazing photo and video shoots, model training, professional hair and makeup services, image consulting, professional wardrobe styling, event planning and production and other specialized workshops fashion events.
With the spaces high ceilings and beautifully lit windows that channel the wonderful natural light of the area you get a special urban feel from the foot traffic outside as if you’re in a New York train station. The space echo’s all things fashion, music and great vibes.
